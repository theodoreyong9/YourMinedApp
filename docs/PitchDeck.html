The Problem

Cryptocurrency participation is broken — networks reward capital deployment over merit, strategic thinking, and community engagement.

Traditional Mining

  • $10,000+ equipment barriers exclude 99% of potential participants
  • Energy consumption creates environmental concerns
  • Centralization around specialized hardware operations
  • Geographic limitations based on electricity costs

Current Alternatives Fall Short

  • Staking: Requires large capital lockup, favors whales
  • DeFi Yield Farming: Complex strategies, impermanent loss risks
  • Airdrops: Uncertain, one-time events with no guaranteed participation
Core Issue: Cryptocurrency networks reward capital deployment over merit, strategic thinking, and community engagement.

The Solution

YourMine — Merit-Based Mining Protocol

⚙️

Burn-to-Earn: Convert SOL tokens into YRM through strategic sacrifice.

Strategic Depth: Multiple variables (timing, tax rates, patience) create optimization opportunities.

Democratic Access: Anyone with internet connection can participate meaningfully.

Dual-Deflationary: Both input (SOL) and output (YRM) experience deflationary pressure.

Core Innovation

A proprietary mathematical formula rewards strategic patience over pure capital, creating sustainable civic yield through gaming mechanics rather than financial speculation.

Key properties
  • Game-like optimization (timing & tax choices)
  • Low barrier to entry — web access only
  • Dual-deflationary mechanics
