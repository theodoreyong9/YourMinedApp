How It Works
1
🔥 Burn SOL
Send SOL to the incinerator. This is your Proof of Sacrifice — permanent, irreversible, on-chain.
2
⚡ Immediate reward
A fraction of YRM is minted immediately based on your burn amount.
3
⏳ Claimable grows
As slots pass, your claimable YRM increases. More patience (τ) = more bonus. Reset resets the clock.
4
♻ Claim & repeat
Claim your YRM anytime. The protocol resets. Burn again to continue mining.
