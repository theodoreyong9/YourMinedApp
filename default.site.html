Building a mine-to-build economic incentive engine — a runtime as light as a simple push. Spheres are plugins. Sites are layers. The formula is the law.
How it works
1
A user burns an amount x of SOL and selects a patience rate T (0–40%).
2
Immediate reward: x(1−T) YM is distributed instantly.
3
The rest becomes a claimable bonus that grows over time — depending only on amount burned, patience rate, and time elapsed.
4
Every burn or claim resets your personal clock. We take 0.1% of each burn to sustain the project.

Why this structure?
Pareto
Economic distributions follow a power law. The protocol integrates this while stabilising it.
Zipf
In self-organised systems, ranks follow a 1/rk hierarchy. Miner hierarchies emerge naturally.
Boltzmann
Exponential distributions are naturally compressed by the logarithm — hence the log compression.
Odum
In energy systems, efficiency decreases with age. A sustainable system must integrate time-based damping.
